Welcome to the Runtao's Game of Life

This is a 3D implementation of Conway's Game of Life with 2333 rules.
This grid is 50x50 and each voxel represents a live cell.
Click on the grid to make a pattern, or, choose from the preset and augment.
Press SHIFT and click to remove a block, and press Start! see life grow!
Once you begin you can use the mouse to explore:
left click to change viewing angle,
middle scroll for zoom,
and right click to change position
